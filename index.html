ABOUT ME

Hello and welcome to my portfolio!

I'm Shrushti Salunke, a Bioinformatics Scientist based in Pune, India. Currently, I'm part of the innovative team at GeneArche Wellness Pvt. Ltd., where we explore exciting intersections of genomics, wellness, and behavioral science.



With a year of hands-on experience in consumer genomics, my work revolves around identifying genetic variations linked to wellness and behavior, aiming to make complex genomic information clear and actionable for everyone. I'm passionate about integrating AI and prompt engineering into healthcare. My toolkit includes Python and Machine learning for automating genomic analyses and HTML/CSS to develop intuitive, user-friendly applications that make data accessible and engaging.



Outside of work, you'll often find me immersed in books, both fictional and non-fictional. As Marathi is my native language, I particularly enjoy classic Marathi literature. I'm also enthusiastic about painting and sketching, finding joy and relaxation through creative arts.
